Controlling Sound

Understanding the 3 ways we make sound work for you

01

Source / Receiver

Develop solution to reduce the noise being propagated from the source (most effective) or at the receiver. These solutions can range from barrier walls to enclosures.

02

Path

Typically implementing a solution of this type will isolate a noisy area of a facility from the quieter area of the plant to mitigate noise exposures of the employees in the quieter areas.

03

Room Acoustics

Is typically utilized in situations where areas of facilities that are very reverberant and need absorption panels/baffles to reduce the reverberant sound. Typically, acoustical engineers will run calculations to determine the amount of absorption necessary in the space to maximize the effectiveness of the sound absorption panels.
