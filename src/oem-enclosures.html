OEM Enclosures

IES-2000, Inc. offers comprehensive OEM solutions, harnessing a diverse array of products to tailor noise control measures precisely to client needs while preserving equipment aesthetics, accessibility, and functionality. From crafting full enclosures to lining vehicle cabs and compartments, and even providing custom wraps, IES-2000's expertise spans the spectrum of noise mitigation strategies.

What sets IES-2000 apart is its capacity to deliver turnkey solutions, encompassing everything from initial design and acoustical engineering to prototyping and seamless installation. The toolbox of solutions includes a versatile range of products such as acoustical foam, curtains, hard panel enclosures, dampening sheets, compounds, and silencers, ensuring a tailored approach to each OEM's noise control challenges. With a commitment to innovation and excellence, IES-2000 stands as a trusted partner in optimizing noise management for OEMs, ensuring both performance and user satisfaction.
