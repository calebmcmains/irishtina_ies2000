Acoustical Enclosures - Modular Systems Application

They are various applications for acoustical enclosures.

Machinery Enclosures

There are machines that tend to produce a lot of noise when operating. You will need soundproof, noise control enclosures to prevent the noise from transmitting to other areas. IES2000 has experienced noise control specialists for sound proof enclosures, acoustical enclosures, and HVAC noise control.

Printing Plant Quiet Rooms

Printing plants involve a high-volume printing operation that produces high sound levels. As a result, it can get very distractive, especially if there are workstations nearby. Our noise control consultants will help prevent this, recommending noise control solutions that will help absorb the noises produced.

Quality Control Test Areas

IES2000 offers building envelope elements for soundproof test rooms, test benches, and test rigs in all the industrial sectors of activity. Our soundproof enclosures can be used in research institutions, engineering schools, and cooperate sectors, among others.

HVAC Noise Control

Our acoustical enclosures and noise reduction enclosures are one of the most reliable HVAC noise control solutions. They help reduce the transmission of noise and sound through modular systems. Flat acoustic foam and duct silencers are commonly used to help decrease sound transmission.

Test Cells

Test cells need minimum sound transmissions from the inner side to the outer side of the test room. They also need to create a specific type of sound propagation. Our modular noise reduction enclosures will help achieve this.
